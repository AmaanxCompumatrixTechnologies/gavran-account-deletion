Account & Data Deletion

Delete Your Account & Personal Data

This page explains how users of the Gavran Android application can request deletion of their account and associated personal data, in accordance with Google Play policies.

How to Request Deletion

Please send an email from your registered email address with the subject "Account Deletion Request" to:

Account Deletion Request

Users can delete their account and associated personal data at any time. To request account deletion, users do not need to reinstall or use the Gavran app.

Account deletion requests can be submitted by sending an email as described below. Once the request is verified, the user account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted.

help.gavran2025@gmail.com

Make sure to include your registered email ID or phone number used in the app so we can verify your request.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User account information
  • Profile and personal details
  • Authentication data (including Google Sign-In)
  • Order and activity data linked to the account

Data Retention

Certain information may be retained temporarily for legal, regulatory, or fraud-prevention purposes. Any retained data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Associated data will be deleted along with the account after the retention period.

Processing Time

All valid deletion requests are processed within 7 working days.

For questions or concerns, contact us at help.gavran2025@gmail.com.
